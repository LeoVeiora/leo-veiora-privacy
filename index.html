Leo Veiora Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 25 August 2025

1. Information We Access

Leo Veiora requests access to certain device features to provide full app functionality:

  • Media Access: We request permission to read your images, videos, and audio files on your device.
    Purpose: To allow you to view, select, and interact with your personal media within the app.
    We do not upload, store, or share your media externally.
  • Vibration Permission: For haptic feedback in the app.

2. How We Use Your Information

Information is used only to enable app features like media previews and user interactions. We do not sell, share, or distribute your data to third parties.

3. Data Storage

Leo Veiora does not store any personal media on servers. All media access happens locally on your device.

4. Third-Party Services

We do not use third-party analytics or advertising in the app.

5. Your Rights

You can revoke permissions anytime in your device settings. If you uninstall the app, all access to your device files is removed.
